FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Official terms and conditions can be found here. The Global Leaderboard Challenge runs from March 19 until April 16, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET.

Each weekly challenges goes live each Thursday, 7:00 AM ET. All previous weekly challenges stay available until April 16.

We will capture the current leaderboard status every Thursday at 7 AM ET. The top 5 players on the Global Leaderboard at that moment will be selected as the potential weekly winners.

Our team requires time to review and approve the potential winners to ensure fair play and eligibility. We aim to announce the confirmed winners publicly each week on Friday (Eastern Time) by adding an Icon next to their names on the leaderboard.

You need to have a valid Epic Games account to participate in the Fortnite Showdown Global Challenge. Join the Challenge by choosing Team Foundation or Team Ice King. Winning rivalries in Fortnite Battle Royale will get you closer to the top of the leaderboard. Each rivalry won is worth 1 point.

In the event of a tie in the leaderboard among top players, pre-defined tiebreakers are already implemented to determine the ranking:
- Victory Royales
- Average Eliminations
- Average Placements
- Average Time Alive

Rewards distribution:
Weekly Winners:
- Top 5 players of Global Leaderboard - NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5080 Founders Edition
End of Challenge Winners:
- Top 20 players of Global Leaderboard - PlayStation 5 console
- Top 100 players of each team (Team Foundation, Team Ice King, Team Creators) - Team Foundation or Team Ice King apparel
- Top 500 players of each team (Team Foundation, Team Ice King, Team Creators) - Fortnite Custom Keychain

NOTE: Each player is eligible to win only one NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5080 Founders Edition. Winning a NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5080 Founders Edition does not affect your eligibility for other prizes.
Epic Games reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to disqualify any participant at any time for any reason, including but not limited to violations of these Official Rules or the Terms of Service.

Physical reward winners will be contacted via email. Make sure your email and mailing address are updated so we can deliver your prize smoothly.

In order to participate in weekly web challenges you need to be registered on the website showdown.fortnite.com. Just by registering on the website you'll receive the Original Rivalry in-game Spray. There are also weekly challenges updated every Thursday. By completing weekly challenges you can earn:
Week 1: Rivaled Runners in-game Kicks
Week 2: Goofyfoot Flail in-game Pickaxe
Week 3: Enticing Pie in-game Emote
Week 4: Eagle's Talons in-game Pickaxe
Weekly challenges can be completed in any order, and simultaneously. All previous weekly challenges stay available for completion throughout the event. If you complete all weekly challenges you'll earn two Emoticons: team Ice King Stay Frosty Emoticon and Team Foundation Gear Up! Emoticon.
Note: the Fortnite Global Showdown Challenge rewards might also be available for purchase in the Item Shop at a later date.

The Challenge is open to individuals who are 13 years of age or older. If you are a minor between 13 years of age and the legal age of majority in the jurisdiction of your residence, you must have permission from a parent or guardian in order to participate in the event.
The Challenge is NOT open to individuals wherever restricted or prohibited by applicable law or any region where participation is prohibited by United States law.

If you no longer want to participate, you can delete your Global Leaderboard Challenge profile on the and turn off public stats in your settings to opt out of the leaderboards.
ATTENTION! If you delete your profile, you will be ineligible to earn the weekly challenge rewards unless you register and complete the challenges again.
